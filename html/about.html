About MRC3D

MRC3D is a small business operating out of Long Island, New York. We supply 3-D Printing Services & Products for both Commercial & Retail Consumers around the Globe. Product catalogs can be found on popular sites & marketplaces including Ebay, Etsy, Mercari, Offerup, Facebook, Instagram, etc.

Starting only in late 2023, MRC3D strives to innovate daily & continue to add a higher quality & quantity of products to our ever growing catalog. Whether it be useful tools / life-hack items, fun toys & figurines & sculptures, custom signs/text, personalized items & jewlery, computer & robotics parts, cosplay & accessories, home decor, special commissioned designs & products to fit your needs (THE LIST GOES ON AND ON), we have a **TON** of different ideas & applications across every field in the world.

The creative possibilities are endless with MRC3D..
